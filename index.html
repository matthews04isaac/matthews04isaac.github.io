Why Choose
Ascend?

Ascend Elite Hockey Development provides an exceptional environment for young players to ascend their skills and reach their full potential. Our programs are designed to challenge athletes and help them grow both on and off the ice.

Our elite-level coaches bring extensive experience and a proven track record of success in advancing players to the next stage of their hockey careers. With backgrounds rooted in competitive hockey and player development, our staff provides the expertise and mentorship athletes need.
