About Us

Built For PlayersWho Want More

Ascend Elite Hockey Development is a Calgary-based organization with one mission: help serious young athletes reach the next level.

Our camps are designed with intentionality — every drill, every rep, every coaching moment is built around developing complete players. We focus on the things that separate good from elite: edge work, decision-making under pressure, compete level, and character.

From first-time camp participants to players chasing junior hockey, Ascend has a program built for where you are and where you want to go.

The Ascend Standard

We hold every player — and every coach — to a high standard from the moment they step on the ice. Effort, attitude, and coachability are non-negotiable. Talent is developed. Character is required.


Our experienced coaches bring backgrounds rooted in competitive hockey and player development. They've played at high levels, coached at high levels, and know exactly what it takes to get there.

Our Approach

Three Pillars of Development

01
⚡

Skill Development

Edge work, puck handling, shooting mechanics, and skating efficiency. We identify and fix the technical gaps holding your game back, then build repeatability under pressure.

02
🧠

Hockey IQ

Reading the play, making decisions at speed, understanding positioning and gap control. Elite players don't just skate — they think. We train the mental game as seriously as the physical.

03
🔥

Leadership & Compete

Compete level, accountability, and leadership on and off the ice. We develop athletes who make their teammates better, handle adversity, and show up every single shift.
